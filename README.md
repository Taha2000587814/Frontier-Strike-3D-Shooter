Frontier Strike: Low Poly Shooter is a fast-paced 3D action game built in Unity using the Low Poly Shooter Pack Free Sample. Designed for both WebGL and Windows, it delivers a streamlined FPS experience that blends arcade-style shooting with tactical survival elements.

Players step into a low-poly frontier battlefield where waves of enemies patrol, chase, and attack. You’ll need to master multiple weapons — from rifles and shotguns to rocket launchers — each with unique firing mechanics and damage profiles. Strategic use of power-ups like health packs, shields, and speed boosts will keep you alive longer as the difficulty ramps up.

The game features a scoring system that tracks your kills and survival time, encouraging replayability and competition. Menus and UI are fully integrated, including a start menu, pause menu, and win/lose screens, ensuring a coherent and polished gameplay loop.
